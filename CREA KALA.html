About Us

Crea-Kala is a service brand under Xperient Technologies Pvt. Ltd., dedicated to providing engineering solutions and advanced R&D across all areas of technology, including civil, mechanical, electrical, and computer science.

As the parent company of Crea-Kala, Xperient Technologies Private Limited is actively developing innovative products such as Metallic Maestro 3D and working on drone technology, currently in the ideation and design phases. The company was established in 2023 by a group of engineering graduates from the Government Engineering College in Mainpuri.
